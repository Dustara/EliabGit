PROJECTS

All projects completed in Roberto Eliab Isaí Rodríguez Rivera's academic trayectory.

Aplication of AR technologies for the teaching of physics.

A computer app for teachers to help them teach physics. In particular, to visualize and navigate 3D models of vectors.



Educat-AR

Web AR page created for the International Education Congress 2019 by petition from Guanajuato's Education Bureau. It's purpose was to visualize 3D models that depicted information about the event and it's participants using a smartphone with internet connection.



AugmentAR Games

Makig use of an Web AR site, combine the experience of playing tabletop games with modern technologies by displaying 3D models above the board using a mobile device with internet connection.
